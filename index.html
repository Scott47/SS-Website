What I like most about software development is how it feels like I am part of a community of learners and problem solvers. I love learning new things, building things, finding solutions, making connections, and being part of team. I like the intrinsic satisfaction that comes with persevering through challenging problems. It's also a people business. Applications and services are built by a community of people, many using open source software and interactions on forums, to serve a need or a want for a community of people. At Nashville Software School, I gained experience with team and individual development projects working in a simulated mock Agile environment. Currently, I am a volunteer teaching assistant with the NSS jumpstart mini-bootcamp program, and building/practicing my coding skills with tutorials, community projects, and personal projects. Right now, I work with Python, Django, Django REST Framework, SQLite, React, JavaScript, HTML 5, CSS3.
